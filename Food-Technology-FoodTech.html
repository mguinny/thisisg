EatDrinX

EatDrinX is founders inaugural project within the Food Tech Industry. This platform is broad in scope and represents a consumer application and industry application. Consumers filter information based on geo-location, while Establishments add/edit/delete information with social sharing integration. EatDrinX had a successful market test and is currently being re-imagined to capitalize on a maturing global market.

EDX Festivals

Leveraging the EatDrinX Platform, EDX Festivals was a spin-off for the Festival industry. To better understand the scope of the Food Technology that was created via EatDrinX, Matt and Pramod partnered with a local festival to explore the viability of a "Vendor Showcase Application for Festivals". Utilizing the 2 applicaiton approach, establishments enter product / service information while consumers use their application to filter information based on geo-location.

Culinary Trail

Barrels, Bottles and Brews is a Passport / Culinary Trail Application. Based on geo-location, users able to discover producers and check-in upon visiting an establishment. Once a specific number of check-ins takes place, the individual is able redeem their activity for a prize. This application has wide spread appeal and can be adapted to various tourism / culinary industries.

Travel Industry

Simplifly is a mistake air-fare applicaiton. Users are able to select their preferred departure cities and add specific destinations to their wish list. A simple user interface, the home screen represents available "deals" based on a users geo-location. Users shall receive push and email notifications as deals meet specific parameters.
