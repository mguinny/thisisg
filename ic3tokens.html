Token Structure

Holding Fungible Value

The IC3 Token is a Lease of Physical Gold, Not to be Confused with a Security. Gold is the oldest form of value and by holding IC3 Tokens, you are holding the physical weight of gold in the form of a verifiable gold backed Token.

Guarantee Token Sale

The Token Holder is the Owner of the Lease. Upon such time when a Token Holder wants to "trade-in" the lease, IC3 Cubes will assure the completion of the transaction at the market settlement - day of sale.

No Sales Tax or Fees

There are No Applicable Taxes or Vaulting Fees. IC3 Tokens represent the convenience of crypto trading with the stability of being priced at the market of gold.

Mainstream Applications

The IC3 Token is structured to ensure stability, longevity and mass adoption, while minimizing all possible external risks for the Token and Token Holders.
