A cryptoasset that is backed by verified physical gold, secured and insured within a private vault and assigned at the time of purchase utilizing blockchain based smart contracts which ensures audit and traceability.

IC3 Cubes will continue to evolve and innovate new methods of investment, trade and exchange for the IC3 Token and cryptoassets as a whole.

FAQ - IC3 Tokens

WHAT IS IC3?

IC3 is a cryptoasset. It’s value is aligned to the settlement price of Gold – COMEX.

One IC3 Token (1 IC3) = 0.00004 (g).

HOW IS THE IC3 ASSET BACKED BY GOLD?

All IC3 Tokens are backed by physical gold in a private vault supported by supply from gold mines located around the world.

All current and future offerings of IC3 Tokens will be supported, secured, verified and insured within this structure. Tokens are only issued off of vaulted gold. Gold bars used to generate tokens shall remain in the vault.

WHERE IS THE PRIVATE VAULT LOCATED?

For security reasons we are unable to disclose the actual location of the vault. IC3 Token holders will be privy to notable information regarding IC3 Tokens and opportunities.

HOW ARE IC3 TOKENS EMITTED?

The amount of IC3 Tokens that are emitted depends on the amount of physical gold available within the current Block. Tokens are assigned to physical gold when purchased, utilizing the smart contract for absolute transparency and auditability.

The amount of IC3 Tokens in circulation can never exceed the market value of physical gold within the vault.

WILL THERE BE ANY AUDITING OF IC3 CUBES ASSETS?

IC3 Cubes has enlisted the auditing services of PWC & Deloitte to audit and ensure that its gold reserves tally with its accounts and with its IC3 Token emission. On top of that, IC3 Cubes has its own IC3 Token internal auditor check its accounts several times a month.
